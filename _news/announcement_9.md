We introduce <a href="https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.24207">Humanline</a> as a **simple yet surprisingly effective** design to **close the online-offline alignment gap** across multiple model families (Llama-3, Gemma-2) and model sizes (1.5B-27B) on both instruction-following and mathematical reasoning.
